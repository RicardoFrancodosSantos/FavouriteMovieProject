The Best Movies According to Ricardo

My top 3 movies of all time.

Fight Club

Fight Club is a critique of consumerism, the modern society's value system, and the pursuit of a false identity through the accumulation of goods.

Top Gun: Maverick

TTop Gun: Maverick is about the importance of human experience and emotional connection in the world of aviation and technology.

Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is about the corruptible nature of power and the fragility of humankind, even within a fantasy world.
